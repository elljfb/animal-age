About the Atlantic Spotted Dolphin

The Atlantic Spotted Dolphin is a member of the Delphinidae family. It is primarily found in Worldwide and inhabits Oceans, Coastal Areas. This animal has a lifespan of 20-25 years and is known for its Gray, White color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Sharks, Orcas. The Atlantic Spotted Dolphin has an average speed of 40-64 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40-64 km/h.

The Atlantic Spotted Dolphin has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 360-380 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
