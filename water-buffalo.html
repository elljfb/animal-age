About the Water Buffalo

The Water Buffalo is a member of the Bovidae family. It is primarily found in Asia, Africa and inhabits Grasslands, Wetlands. This animal has a lifespan of 20-25 years and is known for its Dark Gray color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Tigers, Crocodiles. The Water Buffalo has an average speed of 40 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40 km/h.

The Water Buffalo has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 30-500 offspring after a gestation period of 240-270 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
