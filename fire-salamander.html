About the Fire Salamander

The Fire Salamander is a member of the Salamandridae family. It is primarily found in Europe and inhabits Forests, Caves. This animal has a lifespan of 10-15 years and is known for its Black, Yellow color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Birds, Foxes. The Fire Salamander has an average speed of 0.8 km/h and can reach a top speed of 0.8 km/h.

The Fire Salamander has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 10-60 offspring after a gestation period of 30-60 days. Its conservation status is currently Near Threatened.
