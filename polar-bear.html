About the Polar Bear

The Polar Bear is a member of the Ursidae family. It is primarily found in Arctic and inhabits Arctic. This animal has a lifespan of 25-30 years and is known for its White color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Orcas. The Polar Bear has an average speed of 40 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40 km/h.

The Polar Bear has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-2 offspring after a gestation period of 195-265 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
