About the Tasmanian Devil

The Tasmanian Devil is a member of the Dasyuridae family. It is primarily found in Tasmania, Australia and inhabits Forests, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 5-8 years and is known for its Black, White color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, Scavenger, and it faces threats from Tasmanian Tigers. The Tasmanian Devil has an average speed of 16 km/h and can reach a top speed of 16 km/h.

The Tasmanian Devil has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 20-30 offspring after a gestation period of 21-28 days. Its conservation status is currently Endangered.
