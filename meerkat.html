About the Meerkat

The Meerkat is a member of the Herpestidae family. It is primarily found in Kalahari Desert, Africa and inhabits Arid regions. This animal has a lifespan of 12-14 years and is known for its Brown, Tan color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, Insectivore, and it faces threats from Eagles, Jackals. The Meerkat has an average speed of 32 km/h and can reach a top speed of 32 km/h.

The Meerkat has a Social groups social structure and gives birth to 2-5 offspring after a gestation period of 70-73 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
