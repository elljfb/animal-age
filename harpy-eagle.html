About the Harpy Eagle

The Harpy Eagle is a member of the Accipitridae family. It is primarily found in Central and South America and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 25-35 years and is known for its Black, White color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Jaguars, Snakes. The Harpy Eagle has an average speed of 50 km/h and can reach a top speed of 50 km/h.

The Harpy Eagle has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 56-58 days. Its conservation status is currently Near Threatened.
