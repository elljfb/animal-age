About the Banded Palm Civet

The Banded Palm Civet is a member of the Viverridae family. It is primarily found in Southeast Asia, South Asia and inhabits Forests. This animal has a lifespan of 7-10 years and is known for its Brown, Black, White color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Birds of Prey. The Banded Palm Civet has an average speed of 16 km/h and can reach a top speed of 16 km/h.

The Banded Palm Civet has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 2-5 offspring after a gestation period of 60-64 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
