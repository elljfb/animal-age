About the Shoebill

The Shoebill is a member of the Balaenicipitidae family. It is primarily found in Tropical Central Tropical Africa and inhabits Swamps, Marshes. This animal has a lifespan of 30-35 years and is known for its Grayish-brown color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Crocodiles, Snakes. The Shoebill has an average speed of 14-16 km/h and can reach a top speed of 14-16 km/h.

The Shoebill has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-3 offspring after a gestation period of 30-35 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
