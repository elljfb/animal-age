About the Mandrill

The Mandrill is a member of the Cercopithecidae family. It is primarily found in Central Africa and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 20-40 years and is known for its Brown, Green color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Leopards, Humans. The Mandrill has an average speed of 30 km/h and can reach a top speed of 30 km/h.

The Mandrill has a Social groups social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 175-190 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
