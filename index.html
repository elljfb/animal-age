Frequently Asked Questions

Why do small dogs live longer than large dogs?

Small dogs tend to have slower metabolic rates and experience less stress on their joints and organs, contributing to longer lifespans.

How do cats age compared to dogs?

Cats age rapidly in their first two years, then more slowly. Dogs, especially large breeds, age more quickly throughout their lives.

At what age is a pet considered "senior"?

It varies by species and breed. Cats are typically seniors at 11+, small dogs at 10+, medium dogs at 8+, and large dogs at 6+.

Pet Care Recommendations by Life Stage

  • Puppies/Kittens: Focus on vaccinations, socialization, and a balanced diet.
  • Adults: Maintain regular exercise, dental care, and annual vet checkups.
  • Seniors: Monitor for age-related conditions like arthritis and adjust diet and activity levels as needed.
