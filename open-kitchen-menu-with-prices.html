Open Kitchen is a Chinese & English Food Takeaway in Retford, Nottinghamshire. Open Kitchen is open 6 days a week and allows online orders as well as telephone orders, for both delivery and collection. Dishes include Chicken Dishes, Roast Pork & Beef Dishes, Sweet & Sour Dishes, Seafood Dishes & more.

Open Kitchen Retford Allegry Information

Most of Open Kitchen’s Dishes contain Celery, Cereals containing Gluten, Crustaceans, Egg, Fish, Lupin, Milk, Molluscs, Mustard, Nuts, Peanuts, Sesame Seeds, Soya, Sulphur Dioxide, which some people may have a reaction to. Please inform staff at Open Kitchen before ordering if you suffer from any food allergies. They will do their best to advise you.
