About the Giant Panda

The Giant Panda is a member of the Ursidae family. It is primarily found in China and inhabits Temperate Broadleaf Forests. This animal has a lifespan of 20-30 years and is known for its Black, White color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Snow Leopards. The Giant Panda has an average speed of 32 km/h and can reach a top speed of 32 km/h.

The Giant Panda has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 95-160 days. Its conservation status is currently Endangered.
