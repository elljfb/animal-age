About the Golden Lion Tamarin

The Golden Lion Tamarin is a member of the Callitrichidae family. It is primarily found in Brazil, Southeastern Bahia and inhabits Tropical Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 10-15 years and is known for its Golden, Reddish-brown color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Birds, Snakes. The Golden Lion Tamarin has an average speed of 8-13 km/h and can reach a top speed of 8-13 km/h.

The Golden Lion Tamarin has a Social groups social structure and gives birth to 1-3 offspring after a gestation period of 125-130 days. Its conservation status is currently Endangered.
