About the Markhor

The Markhor is a member of the Bovidae family. It is primarily found in Central Asia, South Asia, Middle East and inhabits Mountains. This animal has a lifespan of 10-13 years and is known for its Brown, Gray color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Wolves, Snow Leopards. The Markhor has an average speed of 40-64 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40-64 km/h.

The Markhor has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-2 offspring after a gestation period of 135-170 days. Its conservation status is currently Near Threatened.
