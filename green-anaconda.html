About the Green Anaconda

The Green Anaconda is a member of the Boidae family. It is primarily found in South America and inhabits Swamps, Marshes. This animal has a lifespan of 10-15 years and is known for its Olive-green color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Jaguars, Caymans. The Green Anaconda has an average speed of 16-24 (in water) km/h and can reach a top speed of 24 km/h.

The Green Anaconda has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to Up to 80 offspring after a gestation period of 150-160 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
