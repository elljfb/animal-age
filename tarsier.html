About the Tarsier

The Tarsier is a member of the Tarsiidae family. It is primarily found in Southeast Asia, Philippines and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 12-20 years and is known for its Gray, Brown color. Its diet consists of Insectivore, and it faces threats from Birds, Snakes. The Tarsier has an average speed of 40-50 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40-50 km/h.

The Tarsier has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 180-210 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
