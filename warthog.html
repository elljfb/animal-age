About the Warthog

The Warthog is a member of the Suidae family. It is primarily found in Sub-Saharan Africa and inhabits Savannahs, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 15-20 years and is known for its Brown, Black color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Lions, Leopards. The Warthog has an average speed of 20 km/h and can reach a top speed of 20 km/h.

The Warthog has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 4-32 offspring after a gestation period of 160-220 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
