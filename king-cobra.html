About the King Cobra

The King Cobra is a member of the Elapidae family. It is primarily found in Southeast Asia, South Asia and inhabits Grasslands, Forests. This animal has a lifespan of 20-30 years and is known for its Olive-green color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Birds, Snakes. The King Cobra has an average speed of 20 km/h and can reach a top speed of 20 km/h.

The King Cobra has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 20-40 offspring after a gestation period of 90-113 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
