About the Sumatran Rhino

The Sumatran Rhino is a member of the Rhinocerotidae family. It is primarily found in Southeast Asia and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 30-45 years and is known for its Reddish-Brown color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Tigers, Humans. The Sumatran Rhino has an average speed of 40 km/h and can reach a top speed of 40 km/h.

The Sumatran Rhino has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 430-640 days. Its conservation status is currently Critically Endangered.
