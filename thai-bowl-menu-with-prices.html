Thai Bowl is a self-proclaimed Premium Chinese and Thai food takeaway in Retford, Nottinghamshire. Thai Bowl is open 6 days a week and serves high quality takeaway food prepared to a restaurant standard such as Thai Soups, Thai Curry Dishes, Chinese & Cantonese Style Dishes etc.

How to Order

You can order from Thai Bowl Retford over the phone or in person at the premises. You can also order online at their website.

Thai Bowl phone number is 01777 703887

Thai Bowl Retford offer collection and delivery options, with a delivery fee of £1.50 within a 2 mile radius. Then £1 Per Mile over 2 miles.

You can order from Thai Bowl online at https://thaibowl.co.uk

Thai Bowl is Cash Only when ordering collection, for Card Payments please order online.

Thai Bowl Retford Allegry Information

Most of Thai Bowls Dishes contain Celery, Cereals containing Gluten, Crustaceans, Egg, Fish, Lupin, Milk, Molluscs, Mustard, Nuts, Peanuts, Sesame Seeds, Soya, Sulphur Dioxide, which some people may have a reaction to. Please inform staff at Thai Bowl before ordering if you suffer from any food allergies. They will do their best to advise you.
