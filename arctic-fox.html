About the Arctic Fox

The Arctic Fox is a member of the Canidae family. It is primarily found in Arctic regions and inhabits Tundra. This animal has a lifespan of 3-6 years and is known for its White color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Polar Bears. The Arctic Fox has an average speed of 60 km/h and can reach a top speed of 60 km/h.

The Arctic Fox has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 4-11 offspring after a gestation period of 52-60 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
