About the Hummingbird

The Hummingbird is a member of the Trochilidae family. It is primarily found in Americas and inhabits Americas. This animal has a lifespan of 3-5 years and is known for its Various color. Its diet consists of Nectar, Insects, and it faces threats from Birds of Prey, Snakes. The Hummingbird has an average speed of 40-80 km/h and can reach a top speed of 80 km/h.

The Hummingbird has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-3 offspring after a gestation period of 13-22 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
