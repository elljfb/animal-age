About the Clownfish

The Clownfish is a member of the Pomacentridae family. It is primarily found in Indian, Pacific Oceans and inhabits Coral Reefs. This animal has a lifespan of 6-10 years and is known for its Orange color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Larger Fish, Anemones. The Clownfish has an average speed of 8 km/h and can reach a top speed of 1.6 km/h.

The Clownfish has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to Up to 1,000 offspring after a gestation period of 6-10 days. Its conservation status is currently Not Evaluated.
