About the Wolf

The Wolf is a member of the Canidae family. It is primarily found in Eurasia, North America, North Africa and inhabits Forests, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 8-13 years and is known for its Gray, White color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Bears, Humans. The Wolf has an average speed of 45 km/h and can reach a top speed of 45 km/h.

The Wolf has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 2-20 offspring after a gestation period of 90-160 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
