About the Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a member of the Canidae family. It is primarily found in Siberia, Alaska and inhabits Cold Regions. This animal has a lifespan of 12-14 years and is known for its Various color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Wolves, Bears. The Siberian Husky has an average speed of 50-65 km/h and can reach a top speed of 48 km/h.
