About the Cheetah

The Cheetah is a member of the Felidae family. It is primarily found in Africa, Iran, India and inhabits Grasslands, Savannas. This animal has a lifespan of 10-12 years and is known for its Tan with Black Spots color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Lions, Hyenas. The Cheetah has an average speed of 112 km/h and can reach a top speed of 112 km/h.

The Cheetah has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 3-5 offspring after a gestation period of 90-98 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
