About the Tree Kangaroo

The Tree Kangaroo is a member of the Macropodidae family. It is primarily found in Papua New Guinea, Indonesia and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 15-20 years and is known for its Brown, Red color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Quolls, Pythons. The Tree Kangaroo has an average speed of 30 km/h and can reach a top speed of 30 km/h.

The Tree Kangaroo has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 126-130 days. Its conservation status is currently Endangered.
