About the Sumatran Tiger

The Sumatran Tiger is a member of the Felidae family. It is primarily found in Sumatra and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 15-20 years and is known for its Orange, Black Stripes color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Leopards, Humans. The Sumatran Tiger has an average speed of 24 km/h and can reach a top speed of 24 km/h.

The Sumatran Tiger has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-5 offspring after a gestation period of 140-230 days. Its conservation status is currently Critically Endangered.
