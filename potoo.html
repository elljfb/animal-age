About the Potoo

The Potoo is a member of the Nyctibiidae family. It is primarily found in Central and South America and inhabits Forests, Woodlands. This animal has a lifespan of 10-15 years and is known for its Gray, Brown color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, Insectivore, and it faces threats from Birds of Prey. The Potoo has an average speed of 16-24 km/h and can reach a top speed of 16-24 km/h.

The Potoo has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 20-30 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
