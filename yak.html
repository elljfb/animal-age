About the Yak

The Yak is a member of the Bovidae family. It is primarily found in Himalayas, Central Asia and inhabits Mountains. This animal has a lifespan of 20-25 years and is known for its Brown, Black color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Snow Leopards, Wolves. The Yak has an average speed of 24 km/h and can reach a top speed of 24 km/h.

The Yak has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 10-50 offspring after a gestation period of 215-280 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
