About the Bottlenose Dolphin

The Bottlenose Dolphin is a member of the Delphinidae family. It is primarily found in Oceans worldwide and inhabits Oceans. This animal has a lifespan of 20-50 years and is known for its Grey color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Sharks, Orcas. The Bottlenose Dolphin has an average speed of 37 km/h and can reach a top speed of 64 km/h.

The Bottlenose Dolphin has a Social pods social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 12 months days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
