About the Giant Pacific Octopus

The Giant Pacific Octopus is a member of the Enteroctopodidae family. It is primarily found in Pacific Ocean and inhabits Ocean Floor. This animal has a lifespan of 3-5 years and is known for its Red, Brown color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Fish, Sharks. The Giant Pacific Octopus has an average speed of 24 km/h and can reach a top speed of 24 km/h.

The Giant Pacific Octopus has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to Up to 200,000 offspring after a gestation period of 50-70 days. Its conservation status is currently Data Deficient.
