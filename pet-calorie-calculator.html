How Our Pet Calorie Calculator Works

Our Pet Calorie Calculator uses scientifically validated formulas to estimate your pet's daily calorie needs. The calculation is based on:

  • Species: Different species have different metabolic rates.
  • Weight: Heavier pets require more calories.
  • Age: Young and senior pets have different energy needs.
  • Activity Level: Active pets burn more calories than sedentary ones.

Tips for Healthy Feeding

  • Measure portions: Use a measuring cup to avoid overfeeding.
  • Choose high-quality food: Look for foods with real meat and minimal fillers.
  • Monitor weight: Regularly check your pet's weight and adjust portions as needed.
  • Consult your vet: Always consult your veterinarian for personalized advice.

Fun Facts About Pet Nutrition

  • Dogs are omnivores and can eat a variety of foods, including fruits and vegetables.
  • Cats are obligate carnivores and require a diet high in animal protein.
  • Rabbits need a diet rich in hay to maintain healthy digestion.
  • Overfeeding is the leading cause of obesity in pets.
