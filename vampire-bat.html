About the Vampire Bat

The Vampire Bat is a member of the Phyllostomidae family. It is primarily found in Americas and inhabits Caves, Forests. This animal has a lifespan of 8-12 years and is known for its Brown color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Hawks, Owls. The Vampire Bat has an average speed of 24-40 km/h and can reach a top speed of 24-40 km/h.

The Vampire Bat has a Colony-based social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 50-70 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
