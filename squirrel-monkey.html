About the Squirrel Monkey

The Squirrel Monkey is a member of the Cebidae family. It is primarily found in Central and South America and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of Up to 20 years and is known for its Yellowish-Orange, Black color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Snakes, Hawks. The Squirrel Monkey has an average speed of 30 km/h and can reach a top speed of 30 km/h.

The Squirrel Monkey has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 40-50 offspring after a gestation period of 15-20 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
