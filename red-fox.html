About the Red Fox

The Red Fox is a member of the Canidae family. It is primarily found in North America, Europe, Asia and inhabits Forests, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 3-5 years and is known for its Reddish-brown color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Wolves, Eagles. The Red Fox has an average speed of 48 km/h and can reach a top speed of 48 km/h.

The Red Fox has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 4-6 offspring after a gestation period of 52 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
