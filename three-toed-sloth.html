About the Three-Toed Sloth

The Three-Toed Sloth is a member of the Bradypodidae family. It is primarily found in Central and South America and inhabits Rainforests. This animal has a lifespan of 10 years and is known for its Brown, Gray color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Harpy Eagles. The Three-Toed Sloth has an average speed of 20 km/h and can reach a top speed of 20 km/h.

The Three-Toed Sloth has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1-2 offspring after a gestation period of 30-40 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
