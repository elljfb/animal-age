About the Echidna

The Echidna is a member of the Tachyglossidae family. It is primarily found in Australia, New Guinea and inhabits Forests, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 15-20 years and is known for its Brown, Tan color. Its diet consists of Insectivore, and it faces threats from Dingoes, Eagles. The Echidna has an average speed of 16-56 km/h and can reach a top speed of 16-56 km/h.

The Echidna has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 22-28 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
