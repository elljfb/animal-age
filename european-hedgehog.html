About the European Hedgehog

The European Hedgehog is a member of the Erinaceidae family. It is primarily found in Europe, Asia and inhabits Gardens, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 2-5 years and is known for its Brown, Spiny color. Its diet consists of Omnivore, and it faces threats from Foxes, Badgers. The European Hedgehog has an average speed of 4-6 km/h and can reach a top speed of 4-6 km/h.

The European Hedgehog has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 3-7 offspring after a gestation period of 35 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
