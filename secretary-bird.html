About the Secretary Bird

The Secretary Bird is a member of the Sagittariidae family. It is primarily found in Africa and inhabits Grasslands, Savannas. This animal has a lifespan of 10-15 years and is known for its Black, Grey color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Snakes, Lizards. The Secretary Bird has an average speed of 24 km/h and can reach a top speed of 32 km/h.

The Secretary Bird has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 2 offspring after a gestation period of 42-46 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
