About the Manatee

The Manatee is a member of the Trichechidae family. It is primarily found in North, Central, and South America and inhabits Rivers, Coastal Areas. This animal has a lifespan of 40-60 years and is known for its Gray, Brown color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Alligators, Sharks. The Manatee has an average speed of 8 km/h and can reach a top speed of 8 km/h.

The Manatee has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 1 offspring after a gestation period of 365-450 days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
