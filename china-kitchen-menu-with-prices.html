China Kitchen Retford Allegry Information

Most of China Kitchen’s Dishes contain Celery, Cereals containing Gluten, Crustaceans, Egg, Fish, Lupin, Milk, Molluscs, Mustard, Nuts, Peanuts, Sesame Seeds, Soya, Sulphur Dioxide, which some people may have a reaction to. Please inform staff at China Kitchen before ordering if you suffer from any food allergies. They will do their best to advise you.
