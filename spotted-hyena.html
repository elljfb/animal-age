About the Spotted Hyena

The Spotted Hyena is a member of the Hyaenidae family. It is primarily found in Sub-Saharan Africa and inhabits Grasslands, Savannahs. This animal has a lifespan of 12-25 years and is known for its Yellowish-brown color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, Scavenger, and it faces threats from Lions, Leopards. The Spotted Hyena has an average speed of 56-64 km/h and can reach a top speed of 56 km/h.

The Spotted Hyena has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 1-4 offspring after a gestation period of 90-110 days. Its conservation status is currently Least Concern.
