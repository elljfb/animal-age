About the Komodo Dragon

The Komodo Dragon is a member of the Varanidae family. It is primarily found in Indonesia and inhabits Islands, Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 20-30 years and is known for its Grey color. Its diet consists of Carnivore, and it faces threats from Humans, Larger Predators. The Komodo Dragon has an average speed of 20 km/h and can reach a top speed of 20 km/h.

The Komodo Dragon has a Solitary social structure and gives birth to 20-25 offspring after a gestation period of 7-8 months days. Its conservation status is currently Vulnerable.
