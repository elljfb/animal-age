Fred's Chinese Menu with Prices

Fred’s Chinese and Cantonese is an Asian Food Takeaway and Restaurant in Retford, Nottinghamshire. Fred’s is open 7 Days a week and serves a mixture of Chinese and Cantonese dishes as well as special set dinners. Opened in 1972, Fred’s Chinese Retford has served the people of Retford for over 50 years.

Fred’s Chinese Retford Allegry Information

Most of Fred’s Dishes contain Celery, Cereals containing Gluten, Crustaceans, Egg, Fish, Lupin, Milk, Molluscs, Mustard, Nuts, Peanuts, Sesame Seeds, Soya, Sulphur Dioxide, which some people may have a reaction to. Please inform staff at Fred’s before ordering if you suffer from any food allergies. They will do their best to advise you.
