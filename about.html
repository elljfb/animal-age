About Retford Report

Your independent voice for local news

Our Mission

Retford Report was founded with a simple but vital mission: to keep the people of Retford informed, engaged, and connected to their community. In an age when local journalism faces unprecedented challenges, we believe that strong, independent local news is more important than ever.

We're committed to providing accurate, balanced reporting on the issues that matter most to Retford residents â€“ from council decisions and planning applications to school achievements, local business success stories, and community events. Our goal is to be the first place people turn when they want to know what's happening in their town.

What We Cover

The Retford Report focuses on news and stories that directly affect our local community:

  • Local Government: Council meetings, planning decisions, and public services
  • Community News: Local groups, charities, and grassroots initiatives
  • Business: High street news, new openings, and local commerce
  • Education: School news, achievements, and educational issues
  • Sport: Local clubs, matches, and sporting achievements
  • Events: What's on in Retford and the surrounding areas
  • History & Heritage: Retford's rich past and its preservation

Our Values

Independence

We're not beholden to any political party, special interest, or corporate owner. Our loyalty is to our readers and the truth.

Accuracy

We take our responsibility to report accurately seriously. Every story is checked and verified before publication.

Community Focus

Retford is our home. We're invested in this town's success and committed to telling stories that reflect our community's diversity.

Transparency

We're open about our processes, corrections, and how we operate. If we make a mistake, we'll own up to it.

Get Involved

The Retford Report belongs to the community it serves. We welcome your news tips, story suggestions, letters to the editor, and feedback. If something's happening in Retford that deserves coverage, we want to hear about it.
