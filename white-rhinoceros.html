About the White Rhinoceros

The White Rhinoceros is a member of the Rhinocerotidae family. It is primarily found in Eastern and Southern Africa and inhabits Grasslands. This animal has a lifespan of 40-50 years and is known for its Gray color. Its diet consists of Herbivore, and it faces threats from Lions, Hyenas. The White Rhinoceros has an average speed of 45 km/h and can reach a top speed of 45 km/h.

The White Rhinoceros has a Group-based social structure and gives birth to 1-14 offspring after a gestation period of 240-300 days. Its conservation status is currently Near Threatened.
