Vote for the Retfordian of the Year 2025!

By Brenda Cooper 20 November 2025, 9:00 AM

Vote for the Retfordian of the Year 2025!

Welcome to the Retfordian of the Year Award 2025!

In the heart of our vibrant community, there are unsung heroes who make Retford an extraordinary place to live. These are the individuals who go above and beyond, offering support, kindness, and inspiration to those around them. Now is your chance to shine a spotlight on someone truly special.

We invite you to nominate and vote for the Retfordian who has made the most significant impact in our local community. Whether it's a dedicated volunteer, a compassionate neighbour, a community leader, or a friend or family member who has shown exceptional spirit, this is your opportunity to celebrate their contributions.

Voting is simple and open to everyone. Cast your vote for the person who embodies the best of Retford – someone who has demonstrated remarkable kindness, courage, or community service throughout the year.

Nominations are now open, and voting will close on Friday 5th December. The winner will be announced shortly after, recognising their outstanding efforts and the positive difference they've made to our community.

Let's come together to honour those who make Retford a truly special place to call home.
